Game Controls

  • Movements: Arrow keys / WASD / ZQSD
  • Place a piece: Enter key, choose with up and down then Enter to confirm the choice
  • Previous move: If you made a mistake press space
  • Restart: Press E or R

Basic Rules

  • If two pieces of the same type are next to each other, they combine.
  • Here is the order of combinations: Circle, Triangle, Square, Quantum
  • You may also notice that when 2 pieces combine, the combination happens where the last piece that moved was (between the 2)
